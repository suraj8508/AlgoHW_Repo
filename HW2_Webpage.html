Malabar Style Biryani

How to Cook Malabar Style Biryani

Ingridients:- (2 Servings)

  • 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon basmati rice
  • 1/4 teaspoon mint leaves
  • 3/4 tablespoon refined oil
  • 1 and 1/4 green cardamom, 3/4 clove
  • Salt (as required)
  • 3/4 onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon hung curd
  • 3/4 tablespoon coriander leaves
  • 1/2 tablespoon ghee
  • Water ( as required)

  • 240 grams chicken
  • 1/2 tablespoon garam masala powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon saffron
  • 1/2 tablespoon bay leaf
  • 1/2 black cardamom
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 and 1/2 green chillies
  • 1/2 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/4 tablespoon ginger
  • 3/4 drops kewra
  • 1/2 tablespoon rose water

Instructions:-

  1. Grind garlic, ginger, and green chilies to a smooth paste. Add little water to make the process easier.
  2. Marinate chicken using half of the paste, ground turmeric, 1 tsp ground red chilies, 1/2 tsp garam masala, and salt.
  3. Keep it in the refrigerator for an hour. Then shallow fry the pieces in oil. Set aside.
  4. Soak 7 cashew nuts in 1/2 cup of water. Set aside for half an hour. Then grind the nuts to a smooth paste. Set aside.
  5. Soak basmati rice in water for half an hour and set aside.
  6. Heat some oil in another pan and fry 1/4 cup of sliced onions, 13 cashew nuts, and raisins separately. Set aside.
  7. To prepare rice for the biriyani:-
    Heat 2 tbsp ghee in a deep pan and splutter fennel seeds. Fry one medium onion until translucent. Add cloves, cardamom pods, cinnamon sticks, and bay leaves. Fry again. Add 8 cups of water along with 3 cups of washed rice. Add lemon juice. Season with salt. Let it come to a boil. Then turn down the heat and cover the pan with a lid. Once cooked, drain and keep it aside.
  8. To prepare gravy for chicken:-
    Heat oil in a pan and fry the rest of the onions until golden brown. Then add the remaining paste and fry for a few seconds. Throw in tomatoes, the rest of the spices ( ground coriander, ground red chilies, ground black pepper, garam masala), and enough salt and cashew nut paste. Fry again for 3-4 minutes. Add chicken pieces along with 1/3 cup of water. Finally, add 2 tsp yogurt and 1 tsp vinegar. Cook again for a couple of minutes.
  9. To arrange the layers:-
    Take a deep heavy bottomed pan and place a layer of chicken. Then add a layer of rice. Pour 1/2 tsp rose water. Use chicken and rice alternatively until finished. But make sure the top layer is rice. Pour 1/2 tsp rose water. Add 1 tbsp ghee.
  10. Now you can garnish biryani with fried cashew nuts, raisins, onions, and chopped coriander leaves.
  11. To 'dum' biryani, place the pan on a large tawa over low heat for 10 minutes.

Tips:-

  • The first and foremost important thing to take care of while preparing chicken biryani recipe is, always use a heavy-bottomed pan as you would not want the chicken getting cooked.
  • The restaurant-style chicken biryani recipe uses the whole chicken in preparation and the chicken breast can dry when cooking at home. It's always suggested to use chicken thigh
  • If you want your chicken to be juicier in your biryani, do not remove the bone.
  • If your cooked rice has turned sticky, spread it on a plate and leave it for 5 minutes. The rice will be separate and fluffy again.
  • Freshly pound spices bring out the best taste in Chicken Biryani Recipe, so make sure that you dry roast all the whole spices for a good 2-3 minutes and then pound or grind them.
